# App Icon Design Prompt for "Bootstrap Your Life"

## App Overview
**App Name:** Bootstrap Your Life  
**Category:** Habit Tracking & Personal Development  
**Tagline:** Daily habits and goals tracking app

## Design Requirements

### Visual Style
- **Modern & Clean:** Minimalist, contemporary design following FutureStyleUI principles
- **Soft & Approachable:** Friendly, inviting aesthetic that encourages daily use
- **Professional yet Warm:** Balance between professional tool and personal companion
- **Gradient-Based:** Use soft gradients as primary visual element
- **Rounded Corners:** Modern, friendly appearance with smooth curves

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- **Purple Gradient:** Soft purple tones (#9B8FA8, muted dusty lavender)
- **Peach Gradient:** Warm peach/coral tones (#C9A882, muted warm beige-orange)
- **Blue Accent:** Muted blue-gray (#6B8FA3)
- **Green Accent:** Success/completion color (soft green)

**Color Characteristics:**
- Muted, desaturated tones (not bright or neon)
- Soft pastels with subtle depth
- Gradient transitions should be smooth and elegant
- Background: Clean white or very light neutral

### Icon Concept Ideas

#### Option 1: Abstract Growth Symbol
"A minimalist icon featuring an upward-growing plant or sprout emerging from a circular base, rendered in soft purple-to-peach gradient. The growth represents habit formation and personal development. Clean, modern lines with rounded edges."

#### Option 2: Checkmark Circle
"A stylized checkmark inside a rounded square or circle, using a soft gradient fill (purple to peach). The checkmark represents completion and achievement. Modern, geometric, with subtle depth through gradient and soft shadow."

#### Option 3: Calendar Check
"A minimalist calendar grid (3-4 days visible) with one day marked by a checkmark or filled circle. Soft gradient background, clean lines. Represents daily habit tracking and consistency."

#### Option 4: Circular Progress Ring
"A circular progress ring (about 75% complete) with a small checkmark or star at the top. Gradient fill from purple to peach. Represents progress, completion, and achievement. Clean, modern, motivational."

#### Option 5: Abstract "B" Letterform
"A stylized letter 'B' (for Bootstrap) with organic, flowing lines. The letter incorporates a checkmark or upward arrow element. Gradient fill purple to peach. Represents brand identity while maintaining habit tracking symbolism."

### Technical Specifications

**Style Guidelines:**
- **Shape:** Rounded square (squircle) or circle
- **Complexity:** Medium - recognizable at small sizes (24x24px)
- **Detail Level:** Moderate - enough detail to be interesting, but simple enough to scale down
- **Depth:** Subtle - soft shadows or gradient depth, not flat
- **Contrast:** Good contrast for visibility on various backgrounds

**Design Principles:**
- Must be recognizable at 24x24px (notification icon size)
- Must work in both light and dark contexts
- Should convey: growth, progress, achievement, daily routine
- Should feel: motivating, friendly, professional, modern
- Should avoid: cluttered, busy, aggressive, childish

### Mood & Emotion
- **Positive:** Uplifting, encouraging, achievement-focused
- **Motivational:** Inspires action and consistency
- **Calm:** Not overwhelming or stressful
- **Trustworthy:** Professional and reliable
- **Personal:** Feels like a personal companion, not just a tool

### Reference Style
- Similar to: Notion, Todoist, Streaks, Habitica (but more modern/soft)
- Design language: Material Design 3, iOS Human Interface Guidelines
- Aesthetic: Modern SaaS apps, wellness apps, productivity tools

- Consider how it will look on various device home screens
- Test at multiple sizes: 24px, 48px, 96px, 512px
- Ensure it stands out but doesn't clash with other apps
- The design should reflect the app's focus on daily habits, consistency, and personal growth
